      Free and easy to use AI for chatting, writing documents and more.

      100% Privacy is guaranteed because this website downloads the AI models, and then processes your data on your own device. Learn more

      The best way to protect your privacy is to never send your data to an AI in the cloud in the first place. Instead, Papeg.ai brings the AI to you, by downloading a complete AI to your device first. This takes a while, but it's worth it!


      • You only need to download an AI once, and it will remain available. Even when the WiFi is switched off.
      • Any documents you edit or create are also stored inside your web browser, on your own device, and not in the cloud.
      • This website does not collect any personal data. It has been developed by Tijmen Schep, a digital artist and privacy designer from Amsterdam.
      • The AI downloads are kindly provided by www.huggingface.co.
